Autonomous
=====
Time Delay: Wait for other robots [left] [right]
Start Location: Left side or right side
Force Ramp: Nothing, press [left]
Cooperation: Press [enter]

Driver
=====
Left Joystick: Left Wheels
Right Joystick: Right Wheels

Operator
=====
Left Joystick: Arm Up/Down
Right Joystick: Should Move Manipulator Up/Down
Left 4-button: Spin Flag Arm

Programs to Load
=====
auto.vi
teleop.vi
debug.vi
Program Chooser.vi
samostat.vi
version.txt
